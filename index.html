I am Assistant Professor of Chemistry at Fresno State (part of California State Univerisy System).

Much of my research has been in organic synthesis of natural products, investigations of new organic reactions and mechanisms, and kinetic studies of isotopically-reinforced sterols involved in peroxidation. I teach undergraduate and graduate courses in organic chemistry. See my Curriculum Vitae for details.
